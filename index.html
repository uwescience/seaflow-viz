SeaFlow dashboard

SeaFlow is a novel underway flow cytometer that continuously measures the abundance and composition of photosynthetic microbial populations.

The dashboard was developed for real-time monitoring of SeaFlow data as it was telemetered from the vessel in real-time. Data are automatically processed on board using our software Popcycle and displayed in 3-minute intervals over the past 25 hours, using SQLShare for data management and D3 for visualization

The SeaFlow project is a collaboration betweeen UW School of Oceanography and eScience which brings together experts in microbial ecology, instrumentation, and data science. This research was sponsored by US National Science Foundation award 1064505, the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation, Microsoft Research, and the Simons Foundation.
